About Us




Deja

Hi, and welcome to Crumbz ‘N Co, my little bakery corner of the internet! I’m Deja, a self-taught baker, and I love trying new and original recipes. Ever since I was a little girl, I was in the kitchen with my Grandmother baking. I have core memories of baking pies and cakes at 4 o’clock in the morning or for parties and gatherings for families and friends. I’ve always been known as the baker or “sweets bringer”. In early 2023, my hobby developed into a small blossoming business, and I couldn’t be happier. I really cherish what I do and every dessert is made with love. I hope you enjoy my sweet treats just as much as I’ve enjoyed making them.
